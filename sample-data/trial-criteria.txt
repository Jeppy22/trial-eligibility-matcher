Inclusion Criteria:

- Male or female adults aged 45 to 75 years at the time of screening.
- Documented diagnosis of type 2 diabetes mellitus for at least 6 months prior to screening.
- HbA1c value between 6.5% and 8.5% (inclusive) at the most recent measurement within the last 90 days.
- On a stable dose of metformin (>= 1000 mg/day) for at least 3 months prior to screening.
- Systolic blood pressure < 160 mmHg and diastolic blood pressure < 100 mmHg at screening.
- Able and willing to provide written informed consent and comply with study procedures.

Exclusion Criteria:

- History of myocardial infarction, stroke, or coronary revascularization within the past 12 months.
- Type 1 diabetes mellitus or any history of diabetic ketoacidosis.
- Estimated glomerular filtration rate (eGFR) less than 45 mL/min/1.73m^2.
- Active malignancy or treatment for malignancy within the past 2 years (excluding non-melanoma skin cancer).
- Current use of insulin, GLP-1 receptor agonists, or SGLT2 inhibitors.
- Pregnancy, lactation, or planning to become pregnant during the study period.
- Known hypersensitivity to the investigational product or any of its excipients.
